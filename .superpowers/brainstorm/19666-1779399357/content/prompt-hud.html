4 · Summoned board

Hidden entirely until you call it (a key, like the ⌘K palette). The terminal stays pristine; the full status board appears laid out in a grid, then folds away. Zero ambient footprint.

This is the real shift — prompt + HUD, not prompt-as-everything. Pick the placement (or two — e.g. a quiet corner HUD always-on plus a summoned full board). Then we design that HUD properly and the prompt becomes genuinely minimal.
